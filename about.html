HOW WE STARTED

Bridging Two Culinary Worlds

Restaurant Canada Best began as a small family dream in Montreal. Our founders, arriving from Malaysia and Sri Lanka, missed the vibrant, sensory-filled open-air night markets of Kuala Lumpur and the lively coastal street stalls of Colombo. They realized Montreal was missing a truly uncompromised representation of these incredible island flavor palettes.

In 2021, they gathered their grandmother's handwritten recipe scrolls, imported a custom-forged heavy iron griddle, and opened our doors. Our goal has always been simple: to serve comfort food that feels like a warm embrace from home, cooked with authentic integrity, zero shortcuts, and extreme passion.

Today, our kitchen has become a culinary melting pot where Montreal locals, homesick students, and passionate foodies assemble to share plates, swap stories, and celebrate the beautiful, spicy culinary bonds of Southeast Asia and the Indian Ocean.

OUR PILLARS

The Secret Behind Our Spices

We believe that great food requires patience, tradition, and premium core foundations. Here is what makes our plates stand out.

🌶️

House-Ground Spices

We never use pre-packaged commercial curry powders. Every single week, we roast whole coriander seeds, cumin, cardamoms, cinnamon, and cloves, pounding them in-house to release their essential, vibrant aromatic oils.

🥥

Fresh Pandan & Coconut

The foundation of Nasi Lemak is coconut milk infused with fresh pandan leaves. We steep real pandan knots and steam our premium long-grain rice multiple times to achieve that signature rich, sweet fragrance.

🧑‍🍳

The Art of the Chop

Kottu Roti is not just a dish—it's a rhythmic performance. Our chefs masterfully chop flaky flatbread, tender meats, and rich curries at blisteringly high heat on a cast iron griddle, blending flavors perfectly inside the shred.
