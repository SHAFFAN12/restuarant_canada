OUR STORY

A Fusion of Spices & Tradition

Bringing the Streets of Kuala Lumpur and Colombo to Montreal

At Restaurant Canada Best, we believe that culinary heritage has the power to connect people across continents. Nestled in the heart of Montreal, our kitchen blends the rich, multi-ethnic flavors of Malaysia with the deep, fiery soul of Sri Lankan street food.

Every plate is crafted using family heritage recipes, house-blended spice packs, and local ingredients. From the rhythm of steel blades slicing Kottu Roti on the griddle to the fragrance of fresh pandan leaves, we invite you to experience a true sensory journey.
